YOUFILTER

Hey! First of all thank you for installing this add-on!
If you found any bugs or want to request a feature please write me an email. Also feel free to leave a rating.
Now on how this works: An icon will appear in your address bar when you navigate to youtube. If you click it a popup will appear. This popup lets you blacklist (or whitelist) a new item and you can edit your items here. The dashboard, which can also be opened in the popup, provides a more convenient way of managing your items. Here you can:
  • Edit an item
  • Delete an item
  • Search your items
  • Change where your data should be saved (more explanation there)
  • Export and import your configuration
  • Activate and Deactivate the filter

The dashboard holds some more information on the settings in detail.
Have fun and keep watching!
